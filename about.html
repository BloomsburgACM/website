Welcome to the ACM student chapter at Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania!

Join us to hang out, participate in hands-on workshops, internship oppurtunities, tutoring, projects, and more! You can also join our programming competition team which travel up and down the east coast to compete in competitions against top schools.

If you enjoy doing things with computers, consider the ACM club! We're an open group, not just for C.S. majors but for anyone interested.
